# App Store Submission Draft

## App name

StudyBuddy

## Subtitle

Exam study planner and quiz coach

## Description

StudyBuddy helps you stay on track while preparing for certification and school exams. Build a daily study rhythm, review objective-based tasks, drill flashcards, and answer original practice prompts with explanations.

The included starter content is tuned for CompTIA A+ Core 1 220-1201, CompTIA A+ Core 2 220-1202, and CompTIA Security+ SY0-701. StudyBuddy includes an active exam picker, separate local progress for each exam, objective-based plans, randomized flashcards, 10-question short tests, randomized 90-question simulations, and PBQ-style matching and drag-to-order scenarios.

StudyBuddy stores progress locally on your device and does not require an account.

StudyBuddy is independent study software. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by CompTIA. CompTIA, A+, and Security+ are trademarks of CompTIA, Inc.

## Review notes

This app provides original study guidance and original practice prompts for selected certification topics. It does not contain real exam questions, copied exam dumps, paid question-bank material, or vendor-restricted content. Scaled scores are study estimates because official exam scoring algorithms and item weights are not public.
